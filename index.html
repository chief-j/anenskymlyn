Welcome to the website of the Hotel Anenský Mlýn 

Would you like to spend a pleasant holiday in the peaceful nature of the Eagle Mountains by Anna’s Brook, right on a cycling trail, which will lead you to the most beautiful surroundings of the locality and have a modern ski centre, perfect for Alpine skiing, snowboarding and cross-country skiing, within striking distance at the same time?

Our hotel is placed at an undisturbed location under the Anna Hill and provides an opportunity for hiking and longer trips or cycling in the summer.

Hotel Anenský Mlýn is barrier free, with a lift.

The ski resort Říčky v Orlických horách is an ideal place for all-day sports activities for visitors of all ages, thanks to a ski school, a snowpark, groomed ski runs and high quality cross-country trails. In the summer, the centre offers tricycle rides and a modern high rope park.

The entire building is non-smoking.
